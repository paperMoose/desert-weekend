where we're staying

Casa Cody

The oldest hotel in Palm Springs — a 1920s adobe hideaway of pink bougainvillea, two saltwater pools, a hot tub, fireplaces, and citrus gardens tucked right at the foot of the mountains. The cutest, most romantic spot in town, and a five-minute walk from dinner and the dance floor.
